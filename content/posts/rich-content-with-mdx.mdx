MDX supports to import React component and embed it directly as markdown content.

## Instagram with react-instagram-embed

<p>
    <InstagramEmbed
        clientAccessToken='<998730790983166>|<5baaa5bc924b50637c74bd5e315a2aeb>'
        url='https://instagr.am/p/Ytlfl/'
        maxWidth={375}
        hideCaption={false}
        containerTagName='div'
        injectScript
        protocol=''
    />
</p>

## YouTube with embed

<div class="video-container"><iframe class="youtube-embed" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/8WEDJVhTiIg" frameBorder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowFullScreen /></div>

## Instagram with iframe and embed url


<iframe class='instagram-post' src="https://www.instagram.com/p/BHisJLwAEeU/embed/captioned/" width="100%" frameBorder='1' />

## YouTube with react-youtube

  <YouTube videoId={"2g811Eo7K8U"} containerClassName={"youtube-container"} />
